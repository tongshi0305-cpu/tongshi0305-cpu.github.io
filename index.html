PolymerFFKit

Scientific Workflow

From trajectories to simulation-ready files

PolymerFFKit organizes the workflow into two modules: trajectory analysis with force-field generation, and sequence design with topology construction.

  1. Map AA trajectories to AA, UA, or CG representations.
  2. Extract bond, angle, and dihedral distributions.
  3. Fit bonded parameters through Boltzmann inversion.
  4. Assign non-bonded parameters and export a unified JSON config.
  5. Generate polymer sequences and export PDB, ITP, TOP, and DATA files.
